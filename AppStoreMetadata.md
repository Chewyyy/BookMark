## Description
BookMark is a personal reading tracker for your EPUB library. Import your books, continue reading where you left off, and keep a clear record of your reading progress over time.

Build a daily reading habit with goals, streaks, a reading calendar, and simple stats that show how much you have read. BookMark keeps the focus on your books instead of recommendations, social feeds, or subscriptions.

Features:

- Import and organize EPUB books
- Continue reading from your library
- Track reading sessions, progress, and finished books
- Set a daily reading goal
- View streaks, calendar history, and reading stats
- Export backups and reading-session data
- Add widgets for quick reading progress at a glance

BookMark is designed for readers who want a private, lightweight place to track their own reading.
